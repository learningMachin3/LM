About

Learning Machine is a two-person team

Learning Machine is a podcast aimed at understanding the American education system by interviewing the researchers and policymakers who are tackling the data that might enlighten us. Each week, we’ll bring you an interview with someone who is actively engaged in this work, and we’ll link to their research so that you can start to understand this system with us.

We are calling on educators of all stripes, academics in the field, policymakers, concerned parents, curious students - join us, be a part of this conversation: help us to understand this learning machine.

Learning Machine is a two human team comprised of:

Nathan is a former computer science teacher and current learning analytics Ph.D. candidate. A bit of a learning machine himself, Nathan enjoys computer programming, dendrology, drinking water with chia seeds in it, and walking his awesome Korean Jindo, BboBbo

Sam is a former middle and high school science teacher in Boston and Denver, as well as a former National Park ranger.Sam is a science-minded creative who likes climbing mountains, playing his guitar, and dancing to LCD Sound System alone on a beach in the moonlight
